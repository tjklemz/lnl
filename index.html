LemonBar

Introducing the XMOS LemonBar, a multi-core embedded system. No Linux. No UNIX. No RTOS. No OS. Just the bare metal.

The LemonBar has up to 16 cores and provides deterministic timing. This means you get the speed and accuracy of true hardware, without the need to deal with FPGAs or ASICs. Plus, you won't have to deal with complex interrupt handling; it's entirely deterministicâ€”just use threads.

Because of the XMOS LemonBar's multi-core architecture, there's no longer a need for an OS to handle things for you. This simplifies your code and means you don't need any libraries at all to get going.
